50-Yard Line

Retransmission

Marketers and consumers alike know that advertising during the Super Bowl is all about generating buzz. In fact, both teams eagerly anticipate the action and energy that come from those great ads! Here is the starting line up of ads that had the best retransmission scores, an Ipsos derived measure that predicts the degree to which ad content gets passed along, or helped along, or otherwise shared in some way by consumers.

Doritos
"Dogs" 		Pantene
"Dad'do" 		Snickers
"Marilyn" 		Mountain Dew Kick Start
"Puppy Monkey Baby" 		Coca Cola
"Mini Marvel" 		Doritos
"Ultrasound" 		Heinz
"Weiner Stampede" 		Colgate
"Save Water" 		Jeep
"Portraits" 		Budweiser
